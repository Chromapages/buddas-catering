## Program Details

- No signup fee
- 12-month commitment period
- Minimum qualifying order: $200 before discount
- Discount applies to qualifying catering orders only
- Cannot be combined with other promotions
- Unused commitments expire at the end of the 12-month term
- Orders must be placed by the enrolled business or organization
- Pay per order as you book — no prepaid subscription required
